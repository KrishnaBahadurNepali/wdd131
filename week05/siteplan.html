Self Help Nepal - Site Plan

Site Name

Self Help Nepal - Supporting orphaned and vulnerable children in Nepal. This name reflects the organization’s mission and is suitable for awareness and outreach purposes.

Site Purpose

This website provides information about Self Help Nepal’s mission, projects, and events. It connects donors and volunteers with opportunities to support orphaned and underprivileged children across Nepal.

Scenarios

  • How can I donate to help children supported by Self Help Nepal?
  • Where can I find the upcoming events or volunteer opportunities?

Color Schema

  • #3E8E7E (Teal): For headings and accents.
  • #F9F9F9 (Light Gray): For background.
  • #333 (Dark Gray): For text.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif - for titles and headings.
  • Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - for general paragraph text.
